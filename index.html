⚠ EMCON — EMISSIONS CONTROL
NO radio transmissions
NO phone calls
NO satellite signals
NO fires (smoke visible for miles)
NO movement during daylight (if hostile area)
MINIMIZE device screen brightness
🏃 EVASION PRIORITIES
1. DISTANCE from crash/ejection site
2. CONCEALMENT — avoid ridgelines, roads
3. DIRECTION — toward friendly lines
4. WATER — find within 24 hours
5. SHELTER — before dark
6. SIGNAL — only when rescue asset confirmed

💧 WATER SOURCES (PRIORITY ORDER)
1. Running streams (uphill from settlements)
2. Morning dew on vegetation
3. Rain collection
4. Snow/ice (melt before drinking)
5. Plant sources (avoid milky sap)

🌡️ HYPOTHERMIA PREVENTION
Stay DRY — wet = 25x faster heat loss
Insulate from GROUND first
Cover HEAD — 40% heat loss from head
CALORIES = heat — eat when available
HUDDLE if multiple survivors
Signs: shivering → confusion → no shiver (DANGER)
